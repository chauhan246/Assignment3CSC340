About Us

We are a privately owned business in Greensboro, NC. Our first shop was opened in 1998 in the downtown area near the court house. With every sell made, we donate 15 % to the local food bank. Our mission is to spread love and build a loving community where we can all enjoy a little bit of chocolate and love for one another. We are recently working on opening a new shop in Raleigh, NC. Please visit us often for updates and promotions coming up.
